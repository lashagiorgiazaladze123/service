The manifesto

პარტიზანი სხვა ისტორიაა

Partizan is a different story. Behind an unmarked door in the old town, it runs three operations at once — barbershop, grooming shop, bar — under one roof and one standard.

No conveyor-belt haircuts. Every session starts with an actual conversation about what you want, runs on classic barbering technique and a straight razor, and ends with a drink in your hand if you want one.

It's the kind of place regulars stop booking ahead for, because the door's always open and the coffee's already coming.
